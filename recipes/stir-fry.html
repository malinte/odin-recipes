Stir-Fry

Description

This vibrant and flavorful stir-fry features tender slices of chicken (or tofu for a vegetarian option) quickly cooked with an array of colorful vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas. The vegetables are crisp-tender, adding a satisfying crunch. It's all tossed in a savory sauce made from soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and a touch of honey for sweetness, balanced with a hint of sesame oil. Serve over steamed rice or noodles for a quick, nutritious meal that's perfect for weeknights, offering a balance of protein, fiber, and fresh flavors.

Ingridients

3-4 servings

For the stir-fry

  • 300g boneless chicken breast (or 300g tofu for vegetarian option), sliced into thin strips
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) vegetable oil (for cooking)
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 150g broccoli florets
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) fresh ginger, minced
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish (optional)
  • 300g cooked jasmine rice or noodles

For the sauce

  • 60ml soy sauce (low-sodium, if preferred)
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) honey or maple syrup (optional for sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon (5g) cornstarch (optional, for thickening the sauce)
  • 100ml water (for the sauce)
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) water (for cornstarch slurry, if using cornstarch)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Slice 300g of boneless chicken breast (or tofu) into thin strips. Slice 1 small onion and both the red and yellow bell peppers. Chop 150g of broccoli florets and 100g of snap peas. Mince 2 garlic cloves and 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger
  2. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together 60ml soy sauce, 1 tablespoon sesame oil, 1 tablespoon honey (if using), 1 tablespoon rice vinegar, and 100ml water. If you prefer a thicker sauce, mix 1 teaspoon cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water to make a slurry. Set aside for later.
  3. Cook the Chicken (or Tofu):Heat 1 tablespoon (15ml) of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken strips and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until cooked through and lightly browned. If using tofu, cook for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden and crisp. Once cooked, remove the chicken or tofu from the pan and set aside.
  4. Stir-fry the Vegetables: n the same pan, add the onion, bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Combine and Add the Sauce:Return the cooked chicken (or tofu) to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the stir-fry, stirring to coat everything evenly. If using the cornstarch slurry, add it now to thicken the sauce, stirring until the sauce has thickened slightly, about 1 minute.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions (optional). Serve immediately over cooked jasmine rice or noodles.
