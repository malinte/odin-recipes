Lemon garlic butter shrimp pasta

Description

This Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta is a light, yet indulgent dish that brings together tender, juicy shrimp with perfectly cooked pasta, all coated in a rich, buttery sauce infused with garlic and a burst of fresh lemon. The shrimp is sautéed until golden and succulent, while the pasta absorbs the flavors of the tangy lemon and savory garlic butter. A hint of red pepper flakes adds a subtle kick, and the fresh parsley brightens the dish with its herbaceous notes. Finished with a sprinkle of Parmesan, this dish strikes the perfect balance between freshness and richness, making it a delightful weeknight dinner or a special meal for guests.

Ingridients

4 servings
  • 225g pasta (spaghetti, linguine, or fettuccine)
  • 450g large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 60g unsalted butter (4 tablespoons)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 60 ml dry white wine (optional, or substitute with chicken broth)
  • 60 ml pasta cooking water (from boiling the pasta)
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 25g grated Parmesan cheese (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook 225g of pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Before draining, reserve about 60 ml of the pasta cooking water, then set the pasta aside.
  2. Cook the shrimp: Season 450g of shrimp with salt and pepper. Heat 2 tablespoons (30 ml) of olive oil and 30g of butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Make the lemon garlic butter sauce: In the same skillet, melt the remaining 30g of butter. Add 4 minced garlic cloves and 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (if using). Sauté for 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in 60 ml of white wine (or chicken broth) and simmer for 2-3 minutes to reduce slightly. Add the juice and zest of 1 lemon, along with the reserved pasta water, stirring to combine.
  4. Combine and toss: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss in the lemon garlic butter sauce. Return the shrimp to the skillet and stir until everything i warmed through and coated with the sauce.
  5. Finish and serve: Remove from heat, stir in 2 tablespoons of chopped parsley, and adjust seasoning with more salt and pepper if necessary. Plate the pasta and shrimp, then sprinkle with 25g of grated Parmesan.
